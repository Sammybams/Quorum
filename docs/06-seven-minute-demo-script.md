# Quorum 7-Minute Demo Script

## 0:00 - 0:40 Opening
- Open `/`
- Say: "Quorum is a student-body operating system for members, meetings, finance, events, and communication."
- Scroll through `Features`, `Pricing`, and the product preview section.

## 0:40 - 1:20 Demo Entry
- Open `/login`
- Click `Explore demo workspace`
- Say: "For judges, we keep a seeded faculty-body demo so they can see a realistic council without needing credentials."

## 1:20 - 2:10 Dashboard
- Land in `Engineering Faculty Council`
- Call out:
  - members count
  - dues health
  - active campaign
  - latest audit report
  - recent activity
- Say: "This is the operating snapshot for the executive council."

## 2:10 - 3:00 Members and Announcements
- Open `Members`
- Show the 7 executive members and role spread
- Open `Announcements`
- Show pinned and published updates
- Say: "Leaders can manage people and internal communication from one place."

## 3:00 - 3:50 Events
- Open `Events`
- Open the town hall event
- Show RSVP and check-in visibility
- Say: "This replaces fragmented event coordination and attendance tracking."

## 3:50 - 4:50 Meetings and AI
- Open `Meetings`
- Open the planning meeting
- Show published minutes and action items
- Say: "Claude turns transcripts into structured minutes and follow-up tasks."

## 4:50 - 5:45 Finance
- Open `Dues`
- Show levy cycle and paid vs pending members
- Open `Campaigns`
- Show streams and confirmed contributions
- Open `Budgets`
- Show planned vs actual budget lines
- Say: "This is where treasury operations become visible instead of spreadsheet-driven."

## 5:45 - 6:20 AI Analytics Report
- Open `Reports`
- Open the seeded semester audit
- Call out:
  - weighted score
  - executive summary
  - category scorecard
  - recommendations for incoming exco
- Say: "Quorum does not just store records. It interprets the leadership cycle and produces a handover-ready audit report."

## 6:20 - 6:45 Links and Integrations
- Open `Links`
- Show short-link tracking
- Open `Settings > Integrations`
- Mention Google connection, Gmail invitations, and meeting sync pipeline

## 6:45 - 7:00 Close
- Return to `Dashboard`
- Say: "Quorum gives student leadership teams a structured system for running real operations, not just posting updates."
